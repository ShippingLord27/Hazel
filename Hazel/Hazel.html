About HAZEL

Connecting people with the things they need through a trusted rental community

Our Story

HAZEL was founded in 2025 with a simple mission: to make renting items as easy as possible. The 4 founders, recognized that many people own items they rarely use, while others need those same items for short periods.

What started as a small local platform has grown into a nationwide rental community with thousands of users. We've helped people save money, reduce waste, and build connections in their communities.

Today, we're proud to be the leading peer-to-peer rental platform, offering everything from power tools to party equipment and more.

Our Mission

To create a sustainable sharing economy by making rental access to goods more convenient and affordable than ownership, while building trust between community members.

Our Vision

We envision a world where people have access to what they need without the burden of ownership, reducing waste and consumption while strengthening local communities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about using HAZEL.

How does HAZEL work?

HAZEL is a peer-to-peer rental platform. Owners list their items for rent, and renters can browse and book these items for a specific duration. We handle secure payments and provide a framework for communication and trust between users.

Is it safe to rent items from others?

Yes. We prioritize safety and trust. We encourage all users to complete our Identity Verification process. Additionally, our review system allows you to see feedback from other users, and our secure messaging system lets you communicate with the owner before you rent.

What happens if an item I rent gets damaged?

Renters are responsible for the items during the rental period. We recommend you communicate with the owner immediately if any damage occurs. Many owners specify their own terms regarding damages and late fees in the listing description. Disputes can be mediated through our support team.

How do I get paid as an owner?

Payments are processed securely through our platform. Once a rental is completed successfully, the earnings (minus our service fee) are transferred to your connected bank account or payment method. You can track your earnings in your profile dashboard.

What is the service fee?

HAZEL charges a small service fee to both the renter and the owner. This fee helps us operate the platform, provide customer support, and continuously improve our services to ensure a safe and reliable experience for everyone.
